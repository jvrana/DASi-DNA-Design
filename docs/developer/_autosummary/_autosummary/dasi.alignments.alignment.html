class dasi.alignments.alignment.Alignment(query_region: dasi.utils.region.Region, subject_region: dasi.utils.region.Region, atype: str, query_key: str, subject_key: str)[source]¶

Bases: collections.abc.Sized

A pair of Regions that ‘aligns’ two regions of DNA sequences. All regions must always be the same length.

A subregion of both regions may be taken.

Makes an alignment between two regions of sequences. Validates the regions are the same length.

Parameters

  • query_region – Query region this alignment aligns to

  • subject_region – Subject region this alignment aligns to.

  • atype – Type of alignment

  • query_key – The record identifier for the query

  • subject_key – The record identifier for the subject
