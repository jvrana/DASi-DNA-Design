dasi.models.Alignment¶

class dasi.models.Alignment(query_region, subject_region, atype, query_key, subject_key, meta=None)[source]¶

Bases: dasi.models.alignment.RepresentsMolecule, collections.abc.Sized

A pair of Regions that ‘aligns’ two regions of DNA sequences. All regions must always be the same length.

A subregion of both regions may be taken.

Makes an alignment between two regions of sequences. Validates the regions are the same length.

Parameters

  • query_region (Region) – Query region this alignment aligns to

  • subject_region (Region) – Subject region this alignment aligns to.

  • atype (str) – Type of alignment

  • query_key (str) – The record identifier for the query

  • subject_key (str) – The record identifier for the subject
